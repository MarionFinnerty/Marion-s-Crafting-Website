Local Crafting Talent

Where can local crafting talent be found and what are they capable of?

There is so much available that you might not be aware of! Every community has a variety of crafts available since there are a number artists from different backgrounds, and all of them have something to bring to the table.

Types of Crafts Available:

  1. Beading
  2. Macrame
  3. Jewelry
  4. Pottery
  5. Woodwork
  6. Metalwork
  7. Glasswork
  8. Knitting
  9. Handicraft

There are also a lot of do it yourself crafting stations all around BC!
